### Project Overview

Working on the GGP commercial required a strategic collaboration between design vision and technical execution. I led the design and art direction for this project while partnering with Jake Wilson from Sleightworks to handle the animation and editing aspects. This collaborative approach allowed us to leverage each other's strengths to create a polished commercial that effectively communicates GGP's value proposition.

### My Role

- **Design Director**: Conceptualized and developed the overall visual design
- **Art Director**: Guided the creative vision and brand consistency
- **Creative Lead**: Established the visual narrative and storytelling approach
- **Collaboration Partner**: Worked closely with Jake Wilson on technical execution

### Collaboration with Jake Wilson

This project featured a strategic partnership with Jake Wilson from Sleightworks:

- **Animation Expertise**: Jake handled the complex motion graphics and animations
- **Technical Execution**: Managed the editing and post-production workflow
- **Creative Partnership**: Collaborated on visual storytelling and pacing
- **Professional Standards**: Ensured broadcast-quality final deliverables

### The Creative Challenge

Creating a commercial for GGP presented unique design challenges:

- **Brand Identity**: Establishing visual consistency with GGP's brand positioning
- **Technical Communication**: Making complex concepts accessible and engaging
- **Visual Hierarchy**: Creating clear information flow and emphasis
- **Motion Integration**: Designing elements that would work effectively in animation
